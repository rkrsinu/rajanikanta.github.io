Research

Molecular Magnetism

My research focuses on molecular magnetism, single-molecule magnets, magnetic anisotropy, zero-field splitting, and spin dynamics in lanthanide and transition-metal complexes.

AI & Machine Learning

I develop graph neural networks, generative AI models, and inverse molecular design strategies for predicting and generating molecular magnetic materials.

Quantum Chemistry

My computational work involves CASSCF, NEVPT2, DFT, ORCA, OpenMolcas, and ab initio electronic structure calculations.
